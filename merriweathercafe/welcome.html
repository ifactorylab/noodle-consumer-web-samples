Merriweather

Merriweather is a cafe designed and owned by Mairi MacKinnon and Josh Russell. We set out to make a warm space that can be enjoyed by anyone who loves tasty food and great coffee.

Currently we are running a simple breakfast menu with lunch specials changing daily. As we settle in more expect our offerings to expand into a bigger lunch menu.

We also have a rooftop garden that we are working on and hope to be growing some of our own produce up there soon.

Coffee

We use coffee from Cup in Woolloongabba. They roast a seasonal blend just for us and we also offer single origin espresso from them.

Baking

We bake every morning and will always have fresh cakes, pastries and muffins on hand.

Food

Seasonal and simple. Our menu is designed with clean, tasty flavours in mind.
